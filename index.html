Here at Font Awesome, it’s summer vacation time, but we don’t let that slow us down. The fa-factory is still humming along shipping improvements and adding more icons.

Font Awesome 6.1.2 is now available!

Here's what's new in 6.1.2:

  • Added compatibility with Vue3 and Nuxt3, along with support for our new animations
  • Made a selection of our animal icons a bit cuter
  • Included support for Ruby on Rails and Sprockets projects with our new font-awesome-pro-sass gem!
  • Updated our React Native component and our free Python package
  • Fixed a “wobble” that could appear with the spin class in Pro Webfont Kits
  • mproved and updated a batch of icons
  • Released the new v6 subsetter for Pro
See What's New in 6.1.2
